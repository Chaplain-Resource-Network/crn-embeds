What the member tools do

Lesson Tutor — a study companion for faster mastery; re-test any concept whenever you want. (Silver+, included)

CE / Credential Navigator — a clear picture of where you stand and the next unit to enroll in, through a confusing credential landscape. (Silver+, included)

Field Care Companion — a fast, cited orientation in your field and a clear refer-or-continue decision, backed by review and a crisis handoff. (Gold+)

Charting / Assessment Assistant — fuller notes drafted faster and a completeness check that surfaces what you might miss (no patient records). (Gold+)

CPE Scenario Trainer — repeatable, low-risk reps on presence, assessment, and referral before a real room. (Gold+)

Multi-Faith Research Assistant + Research Brief Engine — cited, multi-faith research support and on-demand evidence briefs. (Platinum)

Request-a-Chaplain — a fast path from a person in need to a real, qualified chaplain. (included; priority at Platinum)
