What the member tools do

Lesson Tutor — ask it to re-test you on any concept, any time, until it sticks. (Silver+, included)

CE / Credential Navigator — tells you exactly where you stand and the next unit to enroll in, through a confusing credential landscape. (Silver+, included)

Field Care Companion — gives you a fast, cited orientation in your field and a clear refer-or-continue decision, backed by review and a crisis handoff. (Gold+)

Charting / Assessment Assistant — drafts fuller notes faster and flags what you might be missing (no patient records ever stored). (Gold+)

CPE Scenario Trainer — gives you repeatable, low-risk reps on presence, assessment, and referral before a real room. (Gold+)

Multi-Faith Research Assistant + Research Brief Engine — answers cited, multi-faith questions and builds on-demand evidence briefs. (Platinum)

Request-a-Chaplain — routes a person in need to a real, qualified chaplain fast. (included; priority at Platinum)
